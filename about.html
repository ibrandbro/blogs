About

Financial Systems Insights is a personal publishing platform focused on implementation-oriented research across financial crime, risk, payments, compliance architecture, and AI in regulated industries.

The goal is to translate industry and regulatory concepts into practical frameworks that organizations can use to think about systems, data requirements, ownership models, validation design, and operational execution.

This site will continue to publish independent research, implementation blueprints, and industry-specific observations designed for builders, architects, operators, and decision-makers.
